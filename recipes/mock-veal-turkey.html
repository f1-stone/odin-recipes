Mock Veal-turkey

A turkey dish on the run

Quick, easy, tasty, nutritious and low-calorie! Serve with rice and some vegetables. It takes about 25min to make.

Ingredients

  • 1lb turkey breast slices (pounded lightly flour)
  • 1tsp oil
  • 1tsp margarine
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • 1/2tsp thyme
  • 1/2 fresh lemon
  • lemon slices

Steps

  1. Dredge turkey slices in flour
  2. Heat oil and margarine in skillet
  3. Brown turkey
  4. Add mushrooms, onion and thyme as turkey browns
  5. Squeeze lemon juice over turkey slices and remove from skillet
  6. Stir mixture remaining in skillet until blended and slightly thickened
  7. Spoon over turkey slices
  8. Garnish with lemon
