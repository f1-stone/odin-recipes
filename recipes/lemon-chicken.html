Lemon Chicken

How to make a tasty chicken lemon

Fresh lemon juice enhances the flavor of this baked chicken. This can be served with rice, potatoes or some steamed veggies. It is a healthy and nutritious alternative to fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 3lbs chicken (cut up)
  • 2tbs melted margarine
  • 3tbs fresh lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • dash ground pepper
  • 1/2tsp salt (optional)
  • lemon slices

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine margarine, lemon juice, garlic, pepper and salt.
  2. Place chicken in a shallow casserole dish
  3. Pour sauce over chicken
  4. Cover and bake in 350Â°F oven about 45min
  5. Uncover and bake 10min longer
  6. Garnish with lemon slices
