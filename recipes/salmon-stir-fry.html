Salmon Stir-Fry

For fish lovers

This is a quick-to-prepare, low-cholesterol dish. The vegetables stay nice and crisp. Serve with brown rice or quinoa for a healthy lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 16oz salmon
  • 5 cups of sliced vegetables-mushrooms, celery, pepper, carrots
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1tbs oil
  • water
  • 1tbs cornstarch
  • cooked brown rice

Steps

  1. Drain salmon (reserve liquid)
  2. Break salmon into chunks
  3. Stir fry vegetables and garlic in hot oil (1min)
  4. Add water to reserved salmon liquid and cornstarch and blend
  5. Stir mixture into vegetable and cook until slightly thickened
  6. Gently stir in salmon
